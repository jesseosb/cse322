Offroading Axles


Offroading in a vehicle significantly increases your chances of broken parts. The best way to avoid breaking said parts, is to install upgraded versions built to higher specs. This website showcases a few axles that may be used as upgrades in many vehichles.

What is inside an axle, or more specifically, a differential?

The axles above contain ring and pinion gears, "spider gears", axle shafts, bearings, seals, oil and a few other small parts.
